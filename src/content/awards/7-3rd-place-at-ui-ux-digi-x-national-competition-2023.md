## Achievement Overview

Secured **3rd Place** in UI/UX Digi X National Competition 2023, a prestigious design competition hosted by Universitas Padjadjaran (UNPAD), focusing on user interface and user experience design excellence, innovation, and practical application in digital products.

## About Digi X Competition

### Event Overview

Digi X is UNPAD's flagship digital design competition that challenges students to demonstrate mastery of UI/UX design principles, create user-centered designs, and solve real-world problems through thoughtful digital product design.

### Competition Focus

The event emphasizes:

- User-centered design methodology
- Visual design excellence
- Interaction design innovation
- Usability and accessibility
- Design thinking process
- Practical application and impact

## Competition Structure

### Design Challenge

Participants tasked with:

- Analyzing user needs and problems
- Developing comprehensive design solutions
- Creating high-fidelity prototypes
- Demonstrating design process and rationale
- Presenting and defending design decisions

### Evaluation Stages

1. **Initial Submission**: Design proposal and concept
2. **Design Development**: Detailed wireframes and mockups
3. **Prototype Creation**: Interactive high-fidelity prototype
4. **Presentation**: Pitch to judges with live demonstration
5. **Q&A**: Defending design decisions and methodology

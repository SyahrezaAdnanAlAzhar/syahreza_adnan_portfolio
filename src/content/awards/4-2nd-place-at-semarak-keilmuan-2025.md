## Achievement Overview

Secured **2nd Place** in Semarak Keilmuan 2025, a prestigious academic competition hosted by Universitas Swadaya Gunung Jati (UGJ), Cirebon, focusing on scientific excellence and innovation among university students.

## About Semarak Keilmuan

### Event Overview

Semarak Keilmuan is an annual academic competition that celebrates scientific inquiry, research excellence, and innovative thinking among Indonesian university students. The event provides a platform for students to showcase their academic work and compete across various scientific disciplines.

### Competition Scope

The competition encompasses:

- Research presentations
- Scientific innovation showcases
- Academic paper competitions
- Multidisciplinary challenges
- Peer evaluation and expert assessment

## Competition Details

### Event Structure

- **Level**: National university competition
- **Participants**: Students from universities across Indonesia
- **Format**: Presentation and evaluation of scientific work
- **Assessment**: Academic rigor, innovation, presentation quality
- **Duration**: Multi-day competition with various stages

## Achievement Overview

Successfully secured funding for PKM-KI (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa - Karya Inovatif) 2024, a prestigious national student creativity program sponsored by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology of Indonesia.

## About PKM-KI

### Program Description

PKM-KI (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa - Karya Inovatif) is Indonesia's national student creativity program focusing on innovative works that demonstrate creativity, originality, and potential for practical application or commercialization.

### Program Objectives

PKM-KI aims to:

- Foster innovation and entrepreneurial spirit among students
- Encourage creative problem-solving and original thinking
- Develop products or services with market potential
- Bridge academic knowledge with practical applications
- Support student-led innovation initiatives

## Selection Process

### Competitive Evaluation

The PKM-KI program employs rigorous evaluation:

- **Proposal Stage**: Comprehensive written proposal submission
- **Innovation Assessment**: Evaluation of originality and creativity
- **Feasibility Review**: Analysis of implementation potential
- **Expert Panel**: Review by academics and industry professionals
- **Merit-Based Selection**: Only exceptional proposals receive funding

### Evaluation Criteria

Proposals assessed on:

- Innovation and originality of concept
- Potential for implementation and impact
- Team capability and qualifications
- Feasibility and realistic planning
- Clarity of objectives and methodology

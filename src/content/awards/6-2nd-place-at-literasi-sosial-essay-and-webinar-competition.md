## Achievement Overview

Secured **2nd Place** in Literasi Sosial Essay and Webinar Competition organized by Universitas Negeri Semarang (UNNES), demonstrating excellence in social literacy, academic writing, and critical analysis of contemporary social issues.

## About the Competition

### Event Overview

The Literasi Sosial (Social Literacy) Competition is a prestigious academic event that combines essay writing with webinar participation, focusing on promoting social awareness, critical thinking, and academic discourse among Indonesian university students.

### Competition Format

The event consists of two interconnected components:

1. **Essay Writing Competition**: Submission of academic essay on social issues
2. **Webinar Participation**: Engagement with expert speakers on social topics

## Competition Theme

### Social Literacy Focus

The competition addresses:

- Contemporary social issues in Indonesian society
- Social awareness and responsibility
- Critical analysis of social phenomena
- Solutions to social challenges
- Community development and empowerment

### Topics Covered

Areas of focus include:

- Social justice and equality
- Education and literacy
- Community development

- Social innovation
- Cultural awareness
- Sustainable development

## Essay Component

### Writing Requirements

- **Format**: Academic essay with proper structure and citations
- **Length**: Specified word count within academic standards
- **Content**: Original analysis and critical thinking
- **Style**: Formal academic writing with clear argumentation
- **Research**: Evidence-based arguments with proper references

### Essay Structure

- **Introduction**: Context and thesis statement
- **Literature Review**: Existing research and perspectives
- **Analysis**: Critical examination of the issue
- **Arguments**: Well-supported main points
- **Solutions**: Practical recommendations
- **Conclusion**: Summary and implications

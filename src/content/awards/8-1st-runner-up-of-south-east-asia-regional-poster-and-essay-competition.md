## Achievement Overview

Won **1st Runner Up** in the Essay Branch of South-East Asia Regional Poster and Essay Competition 2023, hosted by Universitas Negeri Surabaya (UNESA), a prestigious regional competition attracting participants from across Southeast Asia, focusing on critical regional issues and academic excellence.

## About the Competition

### Event Overview

The South-East Asia Regional Competition is an annual event that brings together talented students from Southeast Asian countries to compete in creative and analytical work addressing regional challenges, opportunities, and cultural themes.

### Competition Scope

- **Geographic Reach**: Southeast Asia region (ASEAN countries)
- **Participants**: University students from multiple Southeast Asian nations
- **Format**: Two branches - Essay Writing and Poster Design
- **Focus**: Regional issues, ASEAN cooperation, sustainable development, cultural understanding

### Significance

- Regional-level competition with international participation
- Platform for youth voices on ASEAN issues
- Promotion of academic excellence and regional cooperation
- Cultural exchange and understanding among ASEAN youth

## Competition Structure

### Two Competition Branches

#### Essay Branch

- Academic essay format
- Research-based argumentation
- Formal writing standards
- Comprehensive analysis of regional topics

#### Poster Branch

- Visual communication design
- Creative representation of concepts
- Information design and aesthetics
- Clear message delivery

## Essay Competition Details

### Competition Requirements

- **Format**: Academic essay with proper structure
- **Length**: Specified word limit for comprehensive treatment
- **Language**: English (for regional accessibility)
- **Content**: Original analysis and critical thinking
- **Research**: Well-supported arguments with credible sources
- **Regional Focus**: Relevant to Southeast Asian context

### Submission Process

1. Topic selection or response to given theme
2. Extensive research and analysis
3. Essay drafting and refinement
4. Multiple revisions for quality
5. Final submission with required formatting
6. Evaluation by expert panel

## Achievement Overview

Successfully secured funding for PKM-KC (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa - Karsa Cipta) 2025, a prestigious national student creativity program sponsored by the Ministry of Higher Education, Science and Technology of Indonesia.

## About PKM-KC

### Program Description

PKM-KC (Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa - Karsa Cipta) is Indonesia's national student creativity program focusing on creating innovative works, products, or technologies that address real-world problems and have potential for commercialization or community impact.

### Selection Process

The PKM-KC program has a highly competitive selection process:

- **Proposal Stage**: Detailed written proposal with innovation concept
- **Evaluation**: Review by expert panels from academia and industry
- **Selection Criteria**: Innovation, feasibility, impact potential, team capability
- **Funding Decision**: Only proposals meeting high standards receive funding
